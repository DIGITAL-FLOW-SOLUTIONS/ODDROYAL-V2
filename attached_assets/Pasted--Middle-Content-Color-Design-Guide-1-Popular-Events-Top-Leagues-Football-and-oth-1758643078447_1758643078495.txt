### 1. **Popular Events / Top Leagues / Football (and other sports) cards**

* **Card Container Background**:
  Use a **middle green shade** (something like `#2F5F3C` or HSL `150 35% 25%`).
  Rounded corners and a subtle shadow to make them pop.
  Same base background across **Popular Events, Top Leagues, Football, Basketball, etc.**

* **Card Border**:
  A slightly **darker green stroke** (`#24492D`, HSL `150 40% 18%`) to create separation from the background.

### 2. **Popular Events â†’ Event Cards**

* **Event Card Background**: Same middle green as parent container but slightly elevated (`#356B43`, HSL `150 35% 28%`).
* **Text Color**: White (`#FFFFFF`) or off-white (`#F1F5F2`) for good contrast.
* **Odds Buttons**:

  * Base: **lighter green shade** (`#4FAF64`, HSL `140 45% 40%`).
  * Hover: **mid-deep green shade** (`#3C8C4D`, HSL `140 45% 30%`).
  * Text inside odds: bold white.

### 3. **Top Leagues Section**

* **League Cards**:
  Background same as popular events (`#356B43`).
  League logos/icons sit inside centered grid.
  Text: white (`#FFFFFF`), with hover accent (`#A8F5B4` or HSL `140 70% 85%`).

* **League Header (gradient)**:
  A smooth gradient bar at the top (e.g. from `#4FAF64` â†’ `#2F5F3C`).

### 5. **Matches inside League Card**

* **Match Row Background**: Even lighter green wash (`#497D56`, HSL `150 30% 35%`).
* **Text**: White for teams, soft gray-green for metadata (time, date).
* **Odds (1 X 2)**:

  * Base: lighter green shade (`#4FAF64`).
  * Hover: mid-deep green shade (`#3C8C4D`).
  * Text: bold white.
  * Subtle shadow on hover for depth.

---

### 6. **Spacing & Shadows**

* **Spacing**: Consistent padding (`16px` outer, `12px` inner\`).
* **Shadows**:

  * Card container: `0 2px 6px rgba(0,0,0,0.25)`.
  * Odds buttons: inset border highlight on hover.
